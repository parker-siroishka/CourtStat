How to Play

Choose 8 players. You can pick any 8 players, however 7 of your players must fit in a starting lineup. The 8th player is a utility player, this player can be any postion and is a strategic player to help you get maximum points.

Your team must include:

Utility Player 1
Setter 1
Libero 1
Middles 2
Outside Hitters 3

These players actions over the season will determine how many points you will acquire. After each week during the season stats will be gathered and your teams total points will be determined.


Points are earned throught the following:

Kills: +1.0
Assists: +0.25
Service Aces: +2.0
Digs: +1.0
Blocks(Solo): +2.0
Blocks(Assists): +1.0
Errors: -1.0
